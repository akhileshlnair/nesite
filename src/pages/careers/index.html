Machine Learning Engineer

Research • Gainesville, FL / Remote • Equity Only

The Role

We are looking for engineers obsessed with efficiency. At Nair Engineering, we aren't training massive models on cloud clusters. We are taking state-of-the-art medical intelligence and compressing it into the secure enclave of a consumer device.

You will work on the bleeding edge of quantization, sparsity, and speculative decoding. Your code will run on the NPU, GPU, and CPU of mobile devices, delivering life-saving insights with 0ms latency and 100% privacy.

About You

You don't need a PhD (though we like them). You need to understand how computers actually work. We prefer students and hackers who have contributed to open-source projects like `llama.cpp` or `tinygrad`.

  • Strong experience with Python and C++.
  • Deep understanding of Transformer architecture internals.
  • Experience with model compression (Quantization, Pruning).
  • Ability to read a paper on Arxiv and implement it over the weekend.

iOS Engineer (Metal)

Engineering • Gainesville, FL / Remote • Equity Only

The Role

Standard iOS development is boring. We aren't building a CRUD app. We are building a high-performance medical instrument. You will be writing Swift that touches the metal. You will be managing memory manually. You will be fighting the scheduler.

The Stack

  • Deep Swift: Unsafe pointers, concurrency, SIMD.
  • Metal: Writing custom shaders for compute tasks.
  • UI: SwiftUI, but pushed to its absolute limits with custom layouts and animations.

Requirements

  • You have built an app that feels "native" but does something impossible.
  • You understand the difference between the main thread and background threads intimately.
  • You care about 120Hz refresh rates.

Systems Engineer

Engineering • Gainesville, FL / Remote • Equity Only

The Role

Our software runs on limited battery and limited thermal headroom. Your job is to make sure the physics of the phone don't get in the way of the intelligence. You will optimize the pipeline from disk read to NPU inference.

About You

You love C++. You hate garbage collection. You know how to read a flame graph. You aren't afraid of allocating your own memory.

  • Mastery of C++17 or newer.
  • Experience with on-device databases (SQLite, Realm) at a low level.
  • Understanding of OS primitives (mutexes, threads, virtual memory).

Product Designer

Design • Gainesville, FL / Remote • Equity Only

The Aesthetic

We do not do "Corporate Memphis." We do not do "Clean SaaS."

We are building the interface for the future of human biology. We want Retrofuturism. Think the interfaces in Alien (1979), Blade Runner, and Evangelion—but functional for a doctor in 2025.

Requirements
